## A moment to end abuse

enditnow® Emphasis Day is our annual opportunity to bring abuse awareness into worship. The August 22 service focuses on educating members and leaders about how to recognize and respond to abuse, raise awareness in the wider Springfield community, and remind survivors that the church is a safe place of care and hope.

We will pray for healing, highlight local resources, and celebrate the faithful response of women and families who have chosen courage over silence.
