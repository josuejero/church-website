## A better way to approach type 2 diabetes

Diabetes Undone Seminar is an 8-week program that helps participants understand type 2 diabetes and take practical steps toward better health through natural, lifestyle-based approaches.

Each session focuses on whole-person wellness, including nutrition, activity, rest, and habits that support long-term health. The seminar is designed for people who want to take control of their health in a clear, practical, faith-aware setting.

The seminar begins on Monday, April 20, 2026 at 7:00 PM at First Springfield Seventh-day Adventist Church and continues over eight weeks.
