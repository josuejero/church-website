## Young Adults Ministry

Young Adults Ministry creates space for emerging adults to grow in faith, purpose, and community during seasons of study, work, service, and transition. We want young adults to build meaningful relationships, deepen their walk with God, and find their place in the life of the church.

Through worship, fellowship, service, and honest conversation, this ministry supports, equips, and encourages young adults to follow Christ with confidence and clarity in everyday life.

Ministry Lead: Anthia Elliott

Assistant Lead: Yolann Guirand

### Programs & Events

- Table Talk happens on the second Sabbath of the month and opens space for practical conversations about faith, work, relationships, calling, and personal growth.
- Seasonal fellowships, service opportunities, and special gatherings are shared through church communications as details are confirmed.
