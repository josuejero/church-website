At the Seventh-day Adventist Church, we believe that true health involves the whole person: body, mind, and spirit. Our Health Ministry is dedicated to promoting practical, faith-based principles that support a vibrant and balanced life.

## Our Health Principles

We encourage a lifestyle that nurtures overall well-being through habits that support daily strength, clarity, and peace.

### Healthy Eating

We emphasize nutritious, whole, plant-based foods that fuel the body and support disease prevention. For practical ideas and meal inspiration, explore [Joy of Eating recipes](https://nadhealth.org/joyofeating/recipes/).

### Regular Exercise

Physical activity helps strengthen the body, improve energy, and support mental health. Even a 20-minute walk after a meal can help your muscles use glucose from the bloodstream for energy, which helps lower blood sugar naturally and improves insulin response.

### Rest and Balance

Adequate sleep and healthy balance allow the body to repair, restore, and recharge. Rest supports hormone regulation, strengthens the immune system, and contributes to mental clarity and emotional health.

### Spiritual Wellness

We encourage a deeper connection with God as the foundation for true healing and peace. We also value the gift of Sabbath rest, a sacred weekly time to pause from daily demands, restore body and mind, and nurture a closer relationship with God.

## The NEWSTART Approach

Our ministry embraces the NEWSTART model, a holistic framework for healthy living:

- Nutrition
- Exercise
- Water
- Sunshine
- Temperance
- Air
- Rest
- Trust in God

These simple but powerful principles work together to help prevent illness, support healing, and promote long-term wellness.

## Our Mission

Through seminars, community programs, and educational resources, we aim to empower individuals and families to make healthier lifestyle choices and experience abundant life.
